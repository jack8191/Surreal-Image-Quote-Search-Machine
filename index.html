Surreal Image/Quote Search Machine

What?

Here's a fun game. Pick a search term. You will find an image based on that search term paired with a random quote. Experement with different terms to see what combinations come up. We've gotten good results with the term 'thumbs' if you want a place to start.

Results

Why and How?

This is a student coding project of me, jack eichner. i undertook it to practice front end and API fundamentals.

i came up with the idea by thinking about how the human mind's ability to find patterns in complex information leads to confusion and befuddlement when it views two things that are not related despite being next to one another.
